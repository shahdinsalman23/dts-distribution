Frequently asked questions

What is DTS Distribution?

DTS Distribution is a trusted logistics and supply chain management company that specializes in providing reliable, efficient, and scalable distribution solutions for businesses across various industries. We help companies streamline their operations, reduce costs, and improve delivery times through our tailored distribution services.

What is the story behind DTS Distribution?

DTS Distribution was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between suppliers and customers through smart, transparent, and dependable logistics. What started as a small regional service has grown into a comprehensive distribution network built on innovation, integrity, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Who manages DTS Distribution?

DTS is led by a team of experienced logistics professionals, supply chain strategists, and customer service experts. Our leadership is dedicated to driving operational excellence, maintaining strong client relationships, and continuously evolving to meet the changing needs of the global marketplace.

What are DTS Distribution’s corporate values?

Our core values include:

  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in every interaction.
  • Reliability: Our clients count on us to deliver on time, every time.
  • Innovation: We leverage the latest technology and logistics trends to stay ahead
  • Customer Focus: Our solutions are built around what our clients truly need.
  • Sustainability: We aim to minimize our environmental footprint through responsible practices.
How does DTS ensure the safety and tracking of shipments?

At DTS Distribution, shipment safety and transparency are our top priorities. We utilize modern tracking technology that allows real-time monitoring of your goods from pickup to delivery. Our vehicles are equipped with GPS, and our operations team maintains constant communication with drivers to ensure route optimization and security. For added peace of mind, we also offer insurance options and instant notifications for key delivery milestones.
